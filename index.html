Harlem Renaissance Tour – Lenox Lounge Stop

This tour stop highlights the historic Lenox Lounge at the southeast corner of 125th Street & Lenox Avenue. Guests learn about its role in jazz, Black culture, and the Harlem Renaissance, and how rising rents and redevelopment led to its closure.

  • Daytime and nighttime storytelling around Lenox Lounge.
  • Context on Billie Holiday, Miles Davis, and Harlem nightlife.
  • Discussion of preservation, gentrification, and cultural memory.
