Bike Tours

Scenic bike rides through Central Park and surrounding areas. Safe, guided, and perfect for seeing more of NYC in less time.

  • Duration: 1–2 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Eco-Friendly: Zero emissions
  • Price: $45–$65 per person

Pedicab Tours

Comfortable, guided pedicab rides through Central Park and Midtown. Great for seniors, families, and anyone who wants a relaxing NYC experience.

  • Duration: 45–90 minutes
  • Difficulty: Very Easy
  • Eco-Friendly: Human-powered
  • Price: $75–$150 per pedicab

Horse & Carriage Tours

Classic NYC carriage rides through Central Park. Romantic, family-friendly, and perfect for special occasions.

  • Duration: 45–60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Very Easy
  • Eco-Friendly: Low impact
  • Price: $120–$180 per carriage
